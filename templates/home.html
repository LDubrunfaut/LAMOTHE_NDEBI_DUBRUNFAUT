Welcome to our secondary structure assignment database

The aim of this database is to store non-redundant (sequence ID < 20%) high resolution ( < 1.6 A) crystal structures from the Protein DataBank (PDB) and to assign protein secondary structures to these proteins using various methods (DSSP and PROSS). The idea is to obtain a secondary structures consensus for each protein - more specifically a consensus of PolyProlines II (PPII) - by aligning secondary structure predictions obtained from different methods. PPIIs are a specific secondary structure that was initially discovered as a series of prolines forming a bend in the backbone. These were thought to be rare, however recent studies show that PPII often do not consist of prolines and thus are actually quite frequent.

This site had been created with Python language using Flask module and Sqlite3 database.
